What is "Systems and Networks"?

Systems and Networks is an area that is very prevalent in the world today. Whether you are accessing this website through WiFi or Ethernet, you are connected to the internet, which is a network. In fact, its a network of networks!

This area of research covers things like how we utilise resources efficiently during high traffic to combat congestion, how networks are structured, the security of the internet, and more. You can read about these research areas and their history on this website.
